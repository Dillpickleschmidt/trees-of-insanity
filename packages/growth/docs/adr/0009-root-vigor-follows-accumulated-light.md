# Root vigor follows accumulated light

The root vigor budget equals the root module's accumulated light capped by the plant type's `v̄_rootmax`. Synthetic Silviculture says accumulated light is redistributed as vigor, omits the older model's proportionality coefficient `α`, and introduces the root cap; treating the omitted coefficient as `1` lets uniform shade reduce total plant vigor instead of only changing allocation ratios.
