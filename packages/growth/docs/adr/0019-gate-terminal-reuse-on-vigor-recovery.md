# Gate terminal reuse on vigor recovery

A terminal vacated by shedding cannot reattach merely because its module-scale vigor remained above `v̄_min`: while empty it must first be observed at or below the threshold, then later rise strictly above it. Never-occupied terminals remain governed by ADR 0018 and attach whenever mature, unoccupied, and vigorous. Synthetic Silviculture does not define reuse; this same-threshold recovery latch is the smallest stateful policy that prevents the observed attach/shed cycles in bundled plant types without adding a cooldown, second threshold, or age protection.
