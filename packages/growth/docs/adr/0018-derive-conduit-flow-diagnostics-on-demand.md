# Derive conduit flow diagnostics on demand

Keep paper-facing module light, vigor, mature-terminal allocation, and growth as simulation truth; edge-level flow through immature modules is derived diagnostic information, not persistent state or a growth input. When requested, distribute each immature module's direct light equally across its developed frontier, accumulate light rootward, and route vigor tipward over the current committed plant conduit network using the simulation's fork policy. This keeps biological routing out of graphics and diagnostics aligned with visible geometry without imposing per-edge diagnostic storage or computation on Ecosystem plants.
