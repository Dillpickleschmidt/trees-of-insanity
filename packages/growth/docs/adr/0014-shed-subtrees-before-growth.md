# Shed below-threshold subtrees before growth

After vigor allocation, a module with vigor strictly below `v̄_min` is shed before age integration or attachment; equality survives. Removing a module removes its attached descendant subtree, and overlapping marks collapse to the highest affected ancestor for one batched topology commit. The root has no special protection, allowing senescence to remove the final module and leave a dead plant rather than letting doomed modules grow or attach during their final step.
